ABOUT PROJECT

2 AND 3 BEDROOM APARTMENTS BANER, PUNE The flawless residential and commercial landmarks built by Rachana Lifestyle are the marks of its top-notch architectural standards and immaculate quality. Having successfully delivered Phase I of Beverly Hills, spread over 3.5 acres, comprising of 3 towers of 12 storeys each, it has set an example in Baner due to its iconic and landmark-worthy structure. Currently, it serves as a home to over 176 happy families. In a quest to combine the finer aspects of aesthetics and functionality that reaches the highest standards of every aspect, we have launched Tower-D of Beverly Hills, which is not only the last of its kind, but the most noteworthy of all. This is a limited-edition tower, with apartments available for selected few. Come, and experience the ideal concoction of design and comfort with Beverly Hills.

With their 3-side open structure lending unrestricted vistas of hill tops disappearing into the clouds and descending sky, the residences enjoy sun filled spaces. The entire project is well appointed in order to meet the day-to-day requirements of the residents.

The latest and final Beverly Hills tower is all about perfections, whether it is the built of the exterior, or the aesthetics and comfort of the interiors. Additionally, there are numerous specifications that would give you a first-hand experience of utmost convenience. The residents get all-inclusive benefits such as ease of access, ample parking space, supreme security and safety for your home, and amenities where the residents can spend their time in pure leisure.
